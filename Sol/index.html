Scale Notes:

  • Planet Radii are approximately to scale with eachother (Mercury=1)
  • Orbit Radii are circular based on the average distance from the sun and are approximately to scale with eachother (Mercury=1)
  • Orbit Speed are constant, based on average orbital velocity, and approximately to scale with eachother (Neptune=1)
  • Orbit Radii are not to scale with Planet Radii

Keyboard Controls

  • E toggle Sun
  • R Reset
  • = Scale orbit radii to planet radii (Don't get lost)
  • WASD or Click+Drag to move
  • Numbers 1-8 teleport to that planets current location (hold to follow)
  • Shift+1-8 set orbit scale to that planet
